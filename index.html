Three apps, one community

Meet your Mahalla

At the heart of mahallify are three connected apps for the people right around you — ask questions, build smaller circles, and find professionals your community trusts.

Community Q&A
Ask Mahalla
Ask anything about life in the neighborhood and let your neighbors answer — visas, schools, the best plov in town. Post openly or anonymously.
  • Real answers from real neighbors
  • Post anonymously when you need to
  • Threaded replies & comments
Small communities
Circles
Join smaller circles built around an interest, building, or cause — and meet the people closest to you. Chat, share a feed, and grow your inner community.
  • Group chats & a shared feed
  • Discover circles near you
  • Create your own in seconds
Recognized people
Professionals
Find trusted professionals recognized by the community — lawyers, doctors, realtors, business owners — or get recognized for your own craft and reach more people.
  • Community-recognized profiles
  • Endorsements from members
  • Get featured for your work

Everything in one place

Built for your community's real needs

From finding your next job to sending a package back home — mahallify covers every corner of everyday community life.

Jobs
Post openings or find work. Browse by category, location, and type — for both employers and job seekers across the US and Canada.
Housing
Find rentals, sublets, roommates, and homes for sale. Filter by price, location, and type — with a map view built in.
Marketplace
Buy and sell within your community — electronics, furniture, clothing, vehicles and more, from people you can trust.
Community Offers
Give and receive rides, food, services, and free items — community-driven generosity made simple.
Travel & Delivery
Traveling to Central Asia? Carry packages for your community. Need something sent home? Find a trusted traveler going your way.
Events
Discover and RSVP to local cultural events, festivals, gatherings, and community meetups near you.
Business Directory
Find immigrant-owned businesses and service providers — with listings, ratings, and reviews from your community.
Smart Location Filters
Browse by state, province, or country. Smart filtering surfaces what's actually relevant — nationwide posts always show.
Direct Messaging
Reach sellers, landlords, and employers directly in the app, with real-time push notifications. Private and trusted.

Sign in your way

Join in seconds — no passwords

Pick whatever's easiest. A one-time code or a single tap and you're in — nothing to create, nothing to remember.

Phone number
Enter your number and verify with a one-time SMS code. Fast, familiar, and secure — no password to set up.
Telegram
Connect your Telegram and sign in with a single tap — no code to type. The fastest way in for the community.
Email
Prefer email? Sign up with your address in under a minute and pick up right where the community is.

🔒 Your contact details stay private — we never post or share them without your say.
