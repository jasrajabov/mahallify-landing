Community Platform
Everything in one place

Built for your community's real needs

From finding your next job to sending a package back home — Mahallify has every corner of community life covered.

Jobs
Post job openings or find work. Browse by category, location, and type. Supports both employers and job seekers across the US and Canada.
Housing
Find rentals, sublets, roommates, and homes for sale. Filter by price, location, and type. Map view included.
Marketplace
Buy and sell items within your community. Electronics, furniture, clothing, vehicles, and more — from people you can trust.
Community Offers
Give and receive rides, food, services, and free items. Community-driven generosity made simple.
Travel & Delivery
Traveling to Central Asia? Carry packages for your community. Need something sent home? Find a trusted traveler going your way.
Events
Discover and RSVP to local cultural events, festivals, gatherings, and community meetups near you.
Business Directory
Find immigrant-owned businesses and service providers. Verified listings, ratings, and reviews from people in your community.
Location Filters
Browse listings by state, province, or country. Smart location filtering shows you what's relevant to you.
Direct Messaging
Contact sellers, landlords, and employers directly within the app. Trusted, private communication.
