How mahallify obtains SMS consent · Use case: account sign-in verification (OTP) · Last updated: May 15, 2026

The opt-in workflow

mahallify sends one type of SMS message only: a one-time verification code (OTP) used to sign a user into their account. There are no marketing, promotional, or recurring messages of any kind.

A user opts in entirely within the mahallify mobile app. On the login screen the user selects "Sign in with Phone," picks their country code, and enters their mobile number. Directly above the "Send Code" button — and separate from acceptance of the Terms of Use or Privacy Policy — the user sees the explicit SMS consent language shown below. Tapping "Send Code" is the affirmative opt-in action and triggers exactly one verification SMS to the number entered.

SMS program disclosure

mahallify SMS Program

Program: mahallify one-time verification codes (OTP) for account sign-in.

Message type: Transactional only. One-time passcodes for authentication. No marketing or promotional messages are ever sent.

Message frequency: One message per sign-in attempt. Not recurring.

Cost: Message and data rates may apply.

To opt out: Reply STOP to any message to cancel. You will receive a confirmation and no further messages will be sent.

For help: Reply HELP or contact us at support@mahallify.us.

Your phone number is never sold or shared with third parties for marketing purposes.
